## What Is Official Translation?

Official translation is the process of translating documents by an authorized translation office for submission to embassies, universities, immigration authorities, courts, and international organizations.

Depending on the destination country, translated documents may also require notarization, legalization, Ministry of Foreign Affairs approval, or embassy authentication.
